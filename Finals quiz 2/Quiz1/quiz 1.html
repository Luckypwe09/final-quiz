My Favorite Place

Siargao

Siargao is a tropical island in the Philippines, located in the province of Surigao del Norte in the Carga region, off the northeastern coast of Mindanao. It's often shaped like a teardrop and is famed worldwide for its ocean waves, natural landscapes, and island-hopping opportunities.

Siargao was originally inhabited by indigenours fishing communities who relied on fishing and farming. Its name came from a mangrove tree that once grew widely on the island.

It is one of my favorite island because its natural beauty and peaceful atmosphere. The Island is known for its clear blue waters, white-sand beaches, and relaxing environment. The warm and friendly locals add to the welcoming feel of the island because of its scenery, relaxed lifestyle, and positive vibe. I always love island-hopping and siargao is my best choice since I always dream to go there; I've always wanted to try surfing specifically on that island since its called as World-class Surfing , especially at Cloud 9.
